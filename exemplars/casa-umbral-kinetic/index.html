Read the estate

We draw slowly.  We build once.  The season  finishes the wine.

Casa Umbral works five parcels on one south slope. No additions, no rush. The grapes ferment on their skins in buried clay, and the angle of the autumn sun that cools the cellar is the same angle that closes the vintage.

Each bottle is the residue of one season of light: the soil it grew in, the wall it warmed against, the long shade it kept through the afternoon. We bottle a place, not a recipe.
